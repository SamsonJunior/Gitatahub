Our Mission

Why We Built GitataHub

Gitata is a vibrant, growing community — but for too long, local information has been scattered across WhatsApp groups, Facebook pages, and word of mouth. Residents struggle to find trusted local businesses, job seekers miss opportunities, and important community announcements reach only a fraction of people.

GitataHub was built to change that. We want every resident of Gitata and Karu LGA to have access to a reliable, up-to-date digital community hub that serves their real daily needs.

We started with Gitata Mirror on Facebook and have now grown into a full digital platform. This is just the beginning.

Community First

Every decision we make puts Gitata residents at the center. This platform is built for you, not for profit alone.

Trusted & Accurate

We verify businesses and fact-check news before publishing. Your trust is our most valuable asset.

Free to Access

Reading news, finding businesses, and browsing events is always free for Gitata residents.

Mobile Friendly

Designed to work perfectly on any phone — because most Gitata residents browse on mobile.
